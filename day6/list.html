DOLO 650
Dolo 650 is an antipyretic/analgesic medicine. It is the medicine usually prescribed to relieve pain relief and control fever. It is used to treat toothaches, headaches, body aches, and the common cold. The medication works by inhibiting the release of natural chemicals that cause pain and fever in the body.
ETOVA MR
Etova Mr is not a general painkiller, it is used as a muscle relaxant. It relieves the pain and discomfort of acute, painful musculoskeletal conditions like rigidity, stress, stiffness and muscle spasms
